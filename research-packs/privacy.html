Research packs privacy

Privacy statement

We keep it practical, minimal, and audit-friendly.

Data use summary: lead intent + attribution to support payment handoff, campaign optimization, and acceptance workflows.

Collected data

  • Email, name, organization, and selected recipe for lead intent.
  • Campaign tags (utm_*) and route/session metadata for funnel analytics.
  • Event telemetry for page and form interactions used to optimize conversion.

Storage and security

  • Data is written to timestamped receipt objects for traceability.
  • Payment credentials are never stored in this landing UI.
  • Access policy is governed by workspace retention and policy controls in the operator path.
