## Independence Statement

There is a moment in every regulated institution when a board member leans forward and asks: *Who decided this was safe enough to ship?*

Noetfield exists for that moment. We are **independent of the application under audit** — we do not inherit Copilot assurances or vendor optimism. We stand beside the decision and produce a receipt that survives scrutiny without asking the audited system to vouch for itself.

We are **independent of value movement** — no funds, custody, settlement, or money transmission. Licensed institutions move value; we move policy, evidence, and traceability.

We are **independent of false certification** — orientation honest; certifier claims only when earned. We do not replace Big Four financial DD or model-risk teams.

We are **independent of hallucinated ops truth** — what is live is written down; what is deferred is named; witness before build.

We are **independent of reckless autonomy** — agents accelerate; founder Hub authorizes outbound. Intelligence without auth is noise.

**In one sentence:** Noetfield is the independent evidence layer for institutional AI decisions — receipts you can defend without asking the app to grade its own homework.
