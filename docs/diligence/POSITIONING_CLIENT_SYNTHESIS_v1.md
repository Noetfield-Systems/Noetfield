## What Noetfield is

- **Governance execution infrastructure** — evaluate operational intent before external systems act
- **Receipt layer** — signed Trust Ledger Entries (TLE v1) with confidence score and board PDF export
- **Metadata-only evidence** — compliance, identity, and audit index — no content custody
- **Shadow-first** — evaluate before enforce; no payment routing or custody claims

## What Noetfield is not

- Not a GRC workflow replacement
- Not a certification or SOC 2 claim
- Not a payment service provider or custody layer
- Not an agent registry or chatbot platform
