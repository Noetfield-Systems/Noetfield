## Pattern notes (internal)

### Positioning (buyer-hero)

- **Pattern:** Governance layer, not AI platform — policy inventory + evidence export for auditors.
- **Noetfield take:** *Receipt layer* — evaluate intent → signed TLE → board PDF.
- **Copy wedge:** "The audit trail your Copilot deployment will be asked for later."

### Copilot complement (registry-vs-receipt)

- **Pattern:** Platform owns registry + policy; partners own tenant configuration + managed enforcement.
- **Noetfield take:** Complement with **pre-execution evaluate + RID lineage** — metadata index in, allow/deny receipt out.
- **Copy wedge:** "Registry tells you what exists; Noetfield tells you what was permitted before it ran."

### MSP two-tier (msp-channel-sow)

- **Pattern:** Tier 1 = CSP configures workspace; Tier 2 = governance pack attach (SOW, office hours, board PDF).
- **Noetfield take:** MSP sells **Copilot Governance Pack** — 90-day pilot SOW, white-label TLE export, GDAP-scoped metadata only.
- **Copy wedge:** "Your clients need Copilot proof — you deliver the receipt, not another dashboard."

### Trust UI (trust-center-grid)

- **Pattern:** Trust center with control status, framework mapping, questionnaire shortcuts — honest about oriented vs certified.
- **Noetfield take:** `/trust-center/` — control checkpoints, framework rows — **not SOC 2 claim**.
- **Copy wedge:** "Continuous verification bundle — shadow stack today, production claims after Form PICK."

### Federal lane (federal-framework-map)

- **Pattern:** AIA + ADM + NIST RMF crosswalk for dual-jurisdiction vendors.
- **Noetfield take:** F lane — TLE export maps to AIA sections + ADM evidence schedule — **no clearance claims**.
- **Copy wedge:** "Pre-execution governance evidence for federal AI inventory — vendor layer only."

### TLE wedge (receipt-export-wedge)

- **Pattern:** Decision receipts, adjudication trails, audit logs — buyers need **export portability** and **RID immutability**.
- **Noetfield take:** TLE v1 = signed go/no-go + confidence score + procurement ZIP + OpenAPI.
- **Copy wedge:** "One receipt your board, auditor, and MSP can all cite — same RID."
