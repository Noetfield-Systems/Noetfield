## Brainstorm — what each benchmark teaches

### Positioning (Credo · Holistic · Veridra · Audital)

- **Pattern:** “Governance layer” not “AI platform” — policy inventory + evidence export for auditors.
- **Noetfield take:** *Receipt layer* — evaluate intent → signed TLE → board PDF. We do not replace GRC workflow tools; we produce the **go/no-go record** procurement asks for.
- **Copy wedge:** “The audit trail your Copilot deployment will be asked for later.”

### Copilot complement (Microsoft · Purview · Inforcer)

- **Pattern:** Microsoft owns registry + platform policy; partners own **tenant configuration + managed enforcement**.
- **Noetfield take:** Complement Agent 365 / Purview with **pre-execution evaluate + RID lineage** — metadata index in, allow/deny receipt out.
- **Copy wedge:** “Registry tells you what exists; Noetfield tells you what was permitted before it ran.”

### MSP two-tier (Inforcer · Cloudiway · AvePoint · Lighthouse)

- **Pattern:** Tier 1 = CSP sells + configures M365; Tier 2 = governance pack attach (SOW, office hours, board PDF).
- **Noetfield take:** MSP sells **Copilot Governance Pack** — 90-day pilot SOW, white-label TLE export, GDAP-scoped metadata only.
- **Copy wedge:** “Your clients need Copilot proof — you deliver the receipt, not another dashboard.”

### www / institutional UI (OneTrust · Vanta · Credo · Drata)

- **Pattern:** Trust center with control status, framework mapping, questionnaire shortcuts — **honest** about what is certified vs oriented.
- **Noetfield take:** `/trust-center/` — control checkpoints, framework rows (NIST · ISO 42001 · EU AI Act orientation), procurement ZIP — **not SOC 2 claim**.
- **Copy wedge:** “Continuous verification bundle — shadow stack today, production claims after Form PICK.”

### Federal lane (Canada AIA · TBS ADM · NIST AI RMF)

- **Pattern:** Algorithmic Impact Assessment + administrative data management + US RMF crosswalk for dual-jurisdiction vendors.
- **Noetfield take:** F lane — TLE export maps to AIA sections + ADM evidence schedule + NIST Govern/Manage — **no clearance claims**.
- **Copy wedge:** “Pre-execution governance evidence for federal AI inventory — vendor layer only.”

### TLE differentiation (Veridra · ADJUDON · Audital · Trinitite)

- **Pattern:** Decision receipts, adjudication trails, audit logs — buyers compare **export portability** and **RID immutability**.
- **Noetfield take:** TLE v1 = signed go/no-go + confidence score + procurement ZIP + OpenAPI — **append-only, RID-keyed, board PDF in one path**.
- **Copy wedge:** “One receipt your board, auditor, and MSP can all cite — same RID.”
